## Evaluation Details

### Rot-13 Encoding
- **Metric Definitions**: 
  - **Encoding Accuracy**: The proportion of sentences correctly encoded using the rot-13 cipher.
  - **Levenshtein Edit Distance**: The number of single-character edits (insertions, deletions, or substitutions) required to change the model's output into the correct encoded sentence.
- **Normalization/Pre-processing**: 
  - Ensure all input sentences are lowercased and punctuation is standardized before encoding.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Calculate per-sentence accuracy and edit distance, then aggregate results by input probability category (high vs. low).
