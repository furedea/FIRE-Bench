## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions and Computation**:
  - Use Pearson correlation, Spearman correlation, and Kendall-Tau correlation to measure the agreement between G-EVAL scores and human judgments.
  - For Spearman correlation, compute the rank order of the scores and compare it to the rank order of human evaluations.
  - For Kendall-Tau correlation, calculate the number of concordant and discordant pairs to assess the rank correlation.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize the scores using the probabilities of output tokens from the LLMs. The final score is computed as a weighted summation of the predefined scores, where the weight is the probability of each score:

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
