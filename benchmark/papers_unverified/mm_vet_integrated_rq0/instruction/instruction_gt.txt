## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions and Computation**:
  - Use an LLM-based evaluator (GPT-4) to score model outputs. The evaluator assigns a score between 0 and 1 based on the correctness and completeness of the response.
  - The scoring prompt includes examples with <AND> and <OR> conditions to handle multiple correct elements in the ground truth.
  - Formula for total score: 
    \[
    S = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^N s_i}{N} \times 100\%
    \]
    where \(s_i\) is the score for sample \(i\), and \(N\) is the total number of samples.
  - Formula for capability-specific score:
    \[
    S_c = \frac{\sum s_i}{N_c} \times 100\%, \quad i \in C
    \]
    where \(C\) is the set of samples requiring a specific capability, and \(N_c\) is the number of such samples.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize model outputs by ensuring they are in a text format suitable for the LLM-based evaluator.
  - Pre-process outputs to match the expected answer style (e.g., short or long form) as per the few-shot examples in the prompt.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Aggregate scores per sample to obtain per-capability scores.
  - Further aggregate per-capability scores to derive an overall score for each model.

## Implementation Notes

- **Key Pitfalls or Caveats**:
