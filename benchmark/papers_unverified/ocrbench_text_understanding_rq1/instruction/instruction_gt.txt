## Experimental Design

### 1. Scene Text-Centric VQA Performance Evaluation
- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the datasets STVQA, TextVQA, OCRVQA, and ESTVQA.
  - For each dataset, select 3000 question instances for testing.
  - Use the prompt "What is written in the image?" for English words and "What are the Chinese characters in the image?" for Chinese words in the ESTVQA dataset.
  - Ensure that questions with answers containing fewer than 4 symbols are filtered out to reduce false positives.
  - Evaluate models: BLIP2-6.7B, mPLUG-Owl, InstructBLIP, LLaVAR, BLIVA, mPLUG-Owl2, LLaVA1.5-7B, UniDoc, DocPedia, Monkey, and Supervised-SOTA.

## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definition**: 
  - Use a simple accuracy metric where the model's output is considered correct if the ground truth answer is present in the output.
  - This approach is chosen over exact matching or ANLS due to the explanatory nature of LMM outputs.
- **Normalization/Pre-processing**: 
  - Filter out questions with answers containing fewer than 4 symbols to avoid false positives.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Calculate accuracy per sample.
  - Aggregate results to obtain per-condition accuracy for each dataset.
  - Compute overall accuracy across all datasets.
