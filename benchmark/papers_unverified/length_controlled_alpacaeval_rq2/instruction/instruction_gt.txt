## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Win Rate**: The expected probability that the auto-evaluator prefers the evaluated model's output over the baseline's output.
  - **Length-Controlled Win Rate**: Computed by removing the length term from the GLM and recalculating the win rate.
  - **Spearman Correlation**: Used to measure the rank correlation between AlpacaEval-LC scores and Chatbot Arena scores.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Standardize the length differences to have unit variance before applying the tanh transformation in the GLM.
  - Ensure that the identity and symmetry properties of the win rate are maintained.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute per-sample win rates, then aggregate to obtain per-condition win rates.
  - Use cross-validation to ensure robustness and avoid overfitting.
