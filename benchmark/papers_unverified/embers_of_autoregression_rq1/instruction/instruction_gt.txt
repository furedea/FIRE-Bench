## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Decoding Accuracy**: The proportion of sentences correctly decoded by the model, where correctness is defined as an exact match with the target output.
  - **Reversal Accuracy**: The proportion of sentences correctly reordered by the model, where correctness is defined as an exact match with the original sentence order.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - For both tasks, ignore quotation marks at the start and end of the model's output.
  - Ensure that the model's output matches the target output exactly, except for permissible variations in punctuation and capitalization as specified in the task instructions.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Calculate per-sample accuracy for each condition.
  - Aggregate results to determine per-condition accuracy.
  - Compare overall accuracy across different output probability conditions to assess the impact of output probability on model performance.

## Implementation Notes

- **Prompt Templates**:

- **Pitfalls and Caveats**:
