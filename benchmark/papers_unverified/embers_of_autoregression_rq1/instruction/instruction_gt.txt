## Experimental Design

### 1. Shift Ciphers: Output Probability
- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the Global Voices dataset to generate sentences with varying probabilities.
  - Encode sentences using the rot-13 cipher.
  - Present the encoded sentences to GPT-3.5 and GPT-4 for decoding.
  - Ensure that the output sentences are categorized into high, medium, and low probability based on their likelihood in natural language as estimated by GPT-2.
- **What to Record**: 
  - Record the decoding accuracy for each probability category.

### 2. Reversal: Output Probability
