## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Accuracy**: Determine if the ground truth answer is present in the model's output. This is a binary metric where the presence of the correct answer in the output is considered a success.
  
- **Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize model outputs by removing any explanatory terms that do not contribute to the answer.
  - Ensure that the outputs are compared against the ground truth answers after filtering out questions with answers containing fewer than 4 symbols.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute accuracy per sample.
  - Aggregate results to obtain per-condition accuracy (i.e., per dataset).
  - Calculate overall accuracy across all datasets to assess general performance.
