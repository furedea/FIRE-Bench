  - Perplexity is calculated as the exponential of the average negative log-likelihood of the test set tokens.
  - Use the formula: \( PPL = \exp\left(-\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}\log p(x_i | x_{<i})\right) \), where \( N \) is the number of tokens.
