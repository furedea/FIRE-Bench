## Evaluation Details

### Multiple-Choice Video Question-Answering (mc-vQA)
- **Metric**: Top-1 accuracy, calculated as the proportion of correctly answered questions.
- **Normalization**: None required.
- **Aggregation**: Compute accuracy per skill area and type of reasoning, then aggregate for overall performance.

### Object Tracking
- **Metric**: Average IoU, calculated as the average overlap between predicted and ground-truth boxes over the entire track.
- **Normalization**: None required.
- **Aggregation**: Compute IoU for each object, then average across all objects.

### Point Tracking
- **Metrics**: 
  - Average Jaccard: Fraction of true positives divided by the sum of true positives, false positives, and false negatives.
  - Occlusion Accuracy: Classification accuracy for point occlusion prediction.
  - Position Accuracy: Fraction of points within a threshold of their ground truth.
- **Normalization**: None required.
- **Aggregation**: Average metrics across all points.

### Temporal Action and Sound Localisation
- **Metric**: Mean Average Precision (mAP), calculated over IoU thresholds.
- **Normalization**: None required.
- **Aggregation**: Average mAP across all classes and IoU thresholds.

### Grounded Video Question-Answering (g-vQA)
- **Metric**: HOTA, integrating detection, association, and localisation scores.
- **Normalization**: None required.
- **Aggregation**: Compute HOTA for each task, then average across all tasks.
