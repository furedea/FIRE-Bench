## Experimental Design

- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the Perception Test dataset.
  - Evaluate the SiamFC model in a zero-shot setting.
  - Track every single object labeled in the dataset starting from an initial bounding box.

## Evaluation Details

### Object Tracking
- **Metric**: Average IoU, calculated as the average intersection-over-union over the entire track between predicted and ground-truth boxes.
- **Normalization**: Ensure bounding boxes are correctly aligned and normalized to the video frame dimensions.

### Point Tracking
- **Metrics**: 
  - Average Jaccard: Fraction of true positives divided by the sum of true positives, false positives, and false negatives.
  - Position Accuracy: Fraction of points within a threshold of their ground truth.
  - Occlusion Accuracy: Classification accuracy for point occlusion prediction.
- **Normalization**: Resize predictions to 256x256 resolution for evaluation.

### Temporal Action Localisation
- **Metric**: Mean Average Precision (mAP) based on temporal IoU between predicted and ground truth segments.
- **Aggregation**: Average mAP across IoU thresholds from 0.1 to 0.5.

### Multiple-choice VideoQA
- **Metric**: Top-1 accuracy, calculated as the fraction of correctly answered questions.
- **Normalization**: Ensure consistent question and answer formatting across all samples.

## Implementation Notes

- **Object Tracking**: Use a heuristic to select a later frame for objects entering the field of view to ensure a representative query box.
- **Point Tracking**: Annotate points densely in time and ensure occluded points are marked correctly.
- **Action Localisation**: Use multimodal inputs by concatenating video and audio features for improved performance.
- **VideoQA**: For Flamingo, use a maximum of 30 frames sampled at 1fps, and ignore the audio modality. For SeViLA, sample 32 frames and use the Localizer to designate keyframes.
