## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Agreement Rate**: Defined as the probability of randomly selected individuals (but not identical) of each type agreeing on a randomly selected question. This is calculated for both tie-inclusive and non-tie scenarios.
  - **Non-tie Agreement Ratio**: The agreement rate calculated only for non-tie votes, providing a clearer picture of alignment when a definitive preference is expressed.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure that all responses are formatted consistently before evaluation.
  - For pairwise comparisons, responses should be presented in a randomized order to mitigate position bias.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Calculate per-sample agreement rates, then aggregate these to determine per-condition agreement rates (e.g., agreement with human experts vs. crowd judges).
  - Finally, compute an overall agreement rate for each evaluation setup.
