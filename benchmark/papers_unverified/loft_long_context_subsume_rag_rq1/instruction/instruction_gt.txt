## Experimental Design

- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the datasets NQ, TopiOCQA, HotPotQA, MuSiQue, QAMPARI, and QUEST.
  - For each dataset, construct a shared corpus by including all gold passages from few-shot, development, and test queries, then sample additional passages uniformly until reaching the desired context size (128k tokens).

## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Subspan Exact Match**: This metric evaluates the exact match of predicted answers with the gold standard answers. For multi-answer datasets, use a linear sum assignment algorithm to match predicted answers to gold answers based on overlap, giving full credit if every gold answer has a perfect match.
  
- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure that the model outputs are formatted as lists of answers for automated evaluation.
  - Use a consistent tokenizer (e.g., SentencePiece) to measure token counts and ensure uniformity across different context lengths.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute the subspan exact match per sample.
  - Aggregate results to obtain per-dataset scores.
  - Calculate the average performance across all datasets to assess overall RAG capability.

## Implementation Notes

- **Prompt Design**:
  - Use Corpus-in-Context (CiC) prompting, which involves providing task-specific instructions, inserting the entire corpus into the prompt, and including few-shot examples with chain-of-thought reasoning.
  - Ensure that each candidate in the corpus is assigned a unique identifier for reference in the model's output.

- **Order of Operations**:
  - First, construct the corpus with gold and random passages.
  - Then, format the prompt with instructions, corpus, and few-shot examples.
  - Finally, execute the model to generate answers and evaluate the outputs.

- **Pitfalls and Caveats**:
  - Be cautious of the model's attention distribution, as performance may degrade if relevant information is positioned towards the end of the corpus.
  - Ensure that the model's outputs are parsed correctly to match the expected answer format for evaluation.
