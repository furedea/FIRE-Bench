## Evaluation Details

### Metric Definitions and Computation
- **Top-1 Accuracy**: 
  - Calculate the percentage of questions where the model's predicted choice matches the ground-truth answer.
- **CircularEval Strategy**: 
  - A model is considered successful on a question only if it correctly predicts the answer in all circular passes.
  - This strategy reduces the impact of random guessing and biases in choice selection.

### Normalization and Pre-processing
- **Choice Extraction**: 
  - Use GPT-4 to extract choice labels from free-form text predictions.
  - If the model's prediction cannot be matched to a choice, return a pseudo choice 'Z'.

### Aggregation Strategy
- **Per-sample to Overall**: 
  - Aggregate results from individual samples to compute per-condition accuracy.
  - Further aggregate per-condition results to obtain overall accuracy for each model.
