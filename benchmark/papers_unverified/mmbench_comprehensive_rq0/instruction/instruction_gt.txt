## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - Accuracy is calculated as the proportion of correctly answered questions out of the total questions for each ability dimension and overall.
  - For CircularEval, a question is considered correctly answered only if the model predicts the correct answer in all circular passes.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Use GPT-4 to normalize model outputs by extracting choice labels from free-form text responses.
  - If the model's prediction cannot be matched to any choice, assign a pseudo choice 'Z'.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Calculate per-sample accuracy first.
  - Aggregate per-condition (ability dimension) accuracy.
  - Compute overall accuracy by averaging across all ability dimensions.
