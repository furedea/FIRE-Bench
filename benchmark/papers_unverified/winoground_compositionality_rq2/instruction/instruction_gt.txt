## Evaluation Details

- **Text Score Calculation**: 
  - For each example, check if the model scores the correct caption higher than the alternative caption for both images.
  - Formula: \( f(C0, I0, C1, I1) = 1 \) if \( s(C0, I0) > s(C1, I0) \) and \( s(C1, I1) > s(C0, I1) \), otherwise 0.

- **Image Score Calculation**: 
  - For each example, check if the model scores the correct image higher than the alternative image for both captions.
  - Formula: \( g(C0, I0, C1, I1) = 1 \) if \( s(C0, I0) > s(C0, I1) \) and \( s(C1, I1) > s(C1, I0) \), otherwise 0.

- **Group Score Calculation**: 
  - Combine text and image scores to evaluate overall correctness.
  - Formula: \( h(C0, I0, C1, I1) = 1 \) if both \( f(C0, I0, C1, I1) \) and \( g(C0, I0, C1, I1) \) are 1, otherwise 0.

- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Compute scores per example, then aggregate scores per tag group, and finally compute overall scores across all examples.
