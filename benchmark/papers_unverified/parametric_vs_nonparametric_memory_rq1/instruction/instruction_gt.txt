## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Accuracy**: A prediction is marked correct if any substring of the prediction is an exact match of any of the gold answers.
  - **Recall@1**: Whether a gold answer is included in the top 1 retrieved document.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize model outputs by stripping whitespace and converting to lowercase before matching with gold answers.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute accuracy per sample, then aggregate to per-condition (e.g., retrieval vs. non-retrieval) and overall accuracy.
