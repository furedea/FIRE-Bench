## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Accuracy (RELAXED)**: A response is credited if the primary answer is correct, even if additional information is outdated or hallucinatory, provided it does not contradict the primary answer.
  - **Accuracy (STRICT)**: A response is credited only if all information is accurate and up-to-date, with no hallucinations.
- **Normalization/Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure that entity names are complete or commonly recognized.
  - Numerical answers must match the ground-truth format unless approximations are explicitly allowed.
- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Calculate per-sample accuracy, then aggregate to per-condition (question type) and overall accuracy.

## Implementation Notes

- **Pitfalls and Caveats**:
