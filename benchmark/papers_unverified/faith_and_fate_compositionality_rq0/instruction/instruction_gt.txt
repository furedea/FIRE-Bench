## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions and Computation**:
  - **Accuracy**: The proportion of correctly solved tasks out of the total number of tasks. Compute separately for in-domain and out-of-domain examples.
  - **Relative Information Gain**: Used to predict surface patterns that models are likely to recognize. Compute as:
    \[
    \text{RelativeIG}(Y_j, X) = \frac{H(Y_j) - H(Y_j|X)}{H(Y_j)}
    \]
    where \(H\) denotes entropy.

- **Normalization/Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize model outputs by ensuring consistent formatting (e.g., removing extraneous whitespace or punctuation) before scoring.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute per-sample accuracy, then aggregate to per-condition (in-domain vs. out-of-domain) and overall accuracy.
