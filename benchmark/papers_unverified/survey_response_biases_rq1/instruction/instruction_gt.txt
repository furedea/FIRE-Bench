- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize the entropy of the answer distributions to measure model uncertainty, using the formula: 
    \[
    \text{Normalized Entropy} = -\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n} p_i \log_2 p_i}{\log_2 n}

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
