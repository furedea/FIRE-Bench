## Evaluation Details

### Metrics
- **Accuracy**: 
  - Measure the proportion of correct evaluations aligning with human judgments.
- **Kappa Correlation Coefficient**: 
  - Compute the agreement between model evaluations and human annotations, accounting for chance agreement.
  - Use the formula: 
    \[
    \kappa = \frac{p_o - p_e}{1 - p_e}
    \]
  where \( p_o \) is the observed agreement and \( p_e \) is the expected agreement by chance.

### Pre-processing and Normalization
- Normalize scores by averaging across multiple evidence samples and positions to mitigate positional bias.

### Aggregation Strategy
- Aggregate scores per sample by averaging across evidence and positions.
- Compute overall metrics by averaging across all samples in the dataset.
