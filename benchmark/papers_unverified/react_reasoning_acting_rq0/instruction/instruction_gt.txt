## Evaluation Details

- **Exact Match (EM)**: 
  - Compute the percentage of predictions that match the ground truth exactly.
  - Use the formula: \( \text{EM} = \frac{\text{Number of exact matches}}{\text{Total number of samples}} \times 100 \).

- **Accuracy**: 
  - Compute the percentage of correctly classified claims in the FEVER dataset.
  - Use the formula: \( \text{Accuracy} = \frac{\text{Number of correct predictions}}{\text{Total number of samples}} \times 100 \).

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**: 
  - Normalize model outputs by trimming whitespace and converting to lowercase before comparison.
  - For FEVER, ensure that the claim's veracity is determined based on retrieved evidence.

- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Compute per-sample metrics first, then aggregate to obtain per-condition and overall scores.
