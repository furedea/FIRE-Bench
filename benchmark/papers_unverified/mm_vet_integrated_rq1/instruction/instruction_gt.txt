- **Metric Definitions**: 
  - The primary metric is the averaged absolute difference (Δ) between LLM-generated scores and human-annotated scores. This is calculated as the mean of the absolute differences for each sample.
  - For the LLM-based evaluation, scores range from 0 (totally wrong) to 1 (totally right), with intermediate scores indicating partial correctness.

## Implementation Notes
