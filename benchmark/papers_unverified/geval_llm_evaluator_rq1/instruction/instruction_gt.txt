## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Spearman Correlation**: Measure the rank correlation between model scores and human judgments.
  - **Kendall-Tau Correlation**: Measure the ordinal association between model scores and human judgments.
  - **Pearson Correlation**: Measure the linear correlation between model scores and human judgments.

- **Normalization and Scoring**:
  - Use the probability of each score token to compute a weighted sum for final scores.
  - Formula: \( \text{score} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} p(s_i) \times s_i \), where \( p(s_i) \) is the probability of score \( s_i \).

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute per-sample scores.
