## Evaluation Details

### Metrics
- **Inclusion of Gold Answers**: Check if the model's output contains the correct answer as a substring.
- **Accuracy**: Compute the proportion of correct answers over the total number of questions.
- **FactScore**: Evaluate the factual precision of generated biographies.
- **str-em**: Measure the exact match of generated answers with the reference.
- **MAUVE**: Assess the fluency of generated text by comparing it to human text.
- **Citation Precision and Recall**: Evaluate the correctness and completeness of citations in the generated text.

### Normalization and Pre-processing
- For fact verification, lowercase model predictions to match gold labels.
- For multiple-choice tasks, ensure model predictions match one of the gold classes by adjusting instructions and conducting string matching.

### Aggregation Strategy
- Compute per-sample metrics (e.g., inclusion of gold answers, accuracy) and aggregate them to obtain per-condition and overall scores.
