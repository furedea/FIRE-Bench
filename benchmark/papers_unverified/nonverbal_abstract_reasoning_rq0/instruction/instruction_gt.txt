## Evaluation Details

### Automatic Scoring Method
- **Metric Definition**: 
  - Use a pattern-matching scheme to identify the number token with the highest logit in the model's response.
  - Compare this token to the ground truth to determine correctness.
- **Normalization**: 
  - Ensure all number tokens are considered, regardless of their position in the response.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Calculate accuracy as the proportion of correctly identified options across all samples.

### Manual Scoring Rubric
