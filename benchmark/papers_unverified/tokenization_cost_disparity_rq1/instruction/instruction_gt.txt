## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Tokenization Premium**: \(|t(sA)|/|t(sB)|\), where \(sA\) is a sentence in language A and \(sB\) is the same sentence in English.
  - **Cost Premium**: Directly proportional to the tokenization premium, assuming a per-token pricing model.
  - **Processing Time**: Measured in seconds for each sentence, plotted against tokenization length.
  - **Context Capacity**: Maximum number of tokens that can be processed by a model, compared across languages.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure all sentences are tokenized using the same tokenizer settings.
  - Normalize tokenization lengths by dividing by the length for English to compute premiums.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute per-sentence tokenization lengths and premiums.
  - Aggregate by language to compute average premiums and processing times.
  - Compare aggregated metrics across languages to assess disparities.
