## Evaluation Details

### Retrieval-Based Context Compression
- **Metrics**: 
  - Use F1 score for QA tasks.
  - Compute F1 as the harmonic mean of precision and recall on the predicted answers compared to the ground truth.
- **Normalization**: 
  - Ensure that the retrieved chunks are concatenated in the order of their relevance scores.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Calculate the average F1 score across all tasks for each retrieval method and chunk size.

### Summarization-Based Context Compression
- **Metrics**: 
  - Use ROUGE-L for summarization tasks.
  - ROUGE-L measures the longest common subsequence between the generated summary and the reference summary.
- **Normalization**: 
  - Ensure that the generated summaries are concatenated in the order they appear in the original context.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Calculate the average ROUGE-L score across all summarization tasks.

## Implementation Notes

- **Retrieval Methods**:
