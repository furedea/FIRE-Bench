## Evaluation Details

- **Mistake-Finding Accuracy**: 
  - Defined as the percentage of traces where the model correctly identifies the first mistake location or correctly indicates no mistakes.
  - Calculated separately for correctmis and incorrectmis traces.

- **Accuracy of Final Answers (accuracyans)**: 
  - Defined as the percentage of traces where the final answer is correct.
  - Changes in accuracyans are recorded as ∆accuracy✓ for originally correctans traces and ∆accuracy✗ for originally incorrectans traces.

- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Mistake-finding accuracy is aggregated per task and per prompting method.
  - Changes in accuracyans are aggregated separately for correctans and incorrectans traces.

## Implementation Notes

- **Prompting Methods**: 
  - Direct trace-level prompting involves using the whole trace as input.
  - Direct step-level prompting involves prompting for each step individually.
  - CoT step-level prompting involves generating a series of reasoning steps for each step.

- **Backtracking Method**: 
  - Regenerate the step at the mistake location with temperature set to 1 until a different step is generated or up to 8 attempts.
  - Continue generating the remaining steps with temperature set to 0.

- **Training Classifier**: 
  - Fine-tune on out-of-domain tasks, holding out one task for evaluation.
  - Use a smaller model (PaLM 2 Otter) for efficiency and interpretability.
