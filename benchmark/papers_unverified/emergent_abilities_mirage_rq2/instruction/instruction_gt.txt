- **Emergence Score Calculation**: 
  - Use the formula provided in the paper:
    \[
    \text{Emergence Score} = \text{sign}(\text{arg max}_i y_i - \text{arg min}_i y_i)(\text{max}_i y_i - \text{min}_i y_i) / \sqrt{\text{Median}(\{(y_i - y_{i-1})^2\}_i)}
