## Evaluation Details

- **Micro Accuracy**: 
  - Formula: \( \text{Acc}_{\text{micro}}(M; P) = \frac{\sum_{p \in P} 1 [M(p) = s(p)]}{|P|} \)
  - Weighs all individual test problems equally.

- **Macro Accuracy**: 
  - Formula: \( \text{Acc}_{\text{macro}}(M; B) = \frac{\sum_{b \in B} 1 [\bigwedge_{p \in P(b)} M(p) = s(p)]}{|B|} \)
  - Considers the fraction of base problems consistently solved across all derived examples.

- **Normalized Accuracy**: 
  - Formula: \( \text{norm}(a_M; M) = \frac{a_M}{n_M} \)
  - Measures how a method is affected by distractors relative to its accuracy on base problems.
