## Evaluation Details

- **Correctness**: Solutions are classified as correct, partially correct, or incorrect based on their ability to perform the task as specified.
- **Security**: Solutions are classified as secure, partially secure, or insecure based on the presence of vulnerabilities such as trivial ciphers, unsafe randomness, SQL injection, and buffer overflows.
- **Metrics**: Use Cohen-Kappa inter-rater reliability to ensure consistency in coding correctness and security.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: Aggregate results per task and compare between experiment and control groups using statistical tests like Chi-squared and logistic regression.
