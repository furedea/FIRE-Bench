  - Use the Compositional Celebrities (CC) dataset to evaluate the compositionality gap.
  - For each question, use a 2-shot prompt with questions from the same category.

### 3. Self-Ask with Search Engine Integration

## Evaluation Details

- **Metrics**: 
  - **Accuracy**: Percentage of correctly answered questions.
  - **F1 Score**: Harmonic mean of precision and recall, used for datasets with multiple possible correct answers.
  - **Cover-EM**: Measures whether the ground truth answer is contained within the generated answer.
- **Normalization**: 
  - Ensure that the final answer is extracted in a short-form format as demonstrated in the prompt.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Calculate per-sample accuracy, then aggregate to per-condition (e.g., self-ask vs. chain of thought), and finally compute overall accuracy across datasets.

## Implementation Notes

- **Prompt Templates**: 
  - Use specific prompt templates for each dataset and method (e.g., direct prompting, chain of thought, self-ask).
  - Ensure prompts demonstrate the expected format of the final answer.
- **Order of Operations**: 
  - For self-ask with search engine, ensure the search engine's response is inserted back into the prompt before the LM continues generating the next follow-up question.
- **Pitfalls**:
