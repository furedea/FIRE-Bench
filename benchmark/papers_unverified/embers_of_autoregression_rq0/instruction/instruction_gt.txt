## Experimental Design

- **Input Format**: Provide the models with sentences encoded in rot-13 and rot-2. Include an example in the prompt to clarify the task.
- **Number of Samples**: 100 sentences per shift level.
- **What to Record**: Record the decoding accuracy for each shift level.

### 2. Pig Latin: Task Probability

## Evaluation Details

### Shift Ciphers
- **Metric**: Decoding accuracy is calculated as the proportion of correctly decoded sentences out of the total number of sentences provided.
- **Normalization**: Ensure that the model outputs are compared against the correct decoded sentences, ignoring any extraneous punctuation or capitalization differences.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: Calculate accuracy per shift level and compare across levels to determine the effect of task probability.

### Pig Latin
- **Metric**: Encoding accuracy is calculated as the proportion of correctly encoded sentences out of the total number of sentences provided.
