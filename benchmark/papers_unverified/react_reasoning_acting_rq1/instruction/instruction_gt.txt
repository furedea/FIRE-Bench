## Implementation Notes

- **Prompt Design**:
  - For ALFWorld, prompts should include sparse thoughts that guide the model through goal decomposition, subgoal tracking, and commonsense reasoning.
  - For WebShop, prompts should include reasoning to bridge the gap between noisy observations and actions.

- **Pitfalls**:
  - Ensure that the model does not get stuck in repetitive loops, particularly in ALFWorld, by carefully designing thoughts to guide the model out of such loops.
  - In WebShop, ensure that the model correctly identifies and selects product options that match the user instructions.

- **Order of Operations**:
  - In ALFWorld, the sequence of actions should follow the logical order of finding, cleaning, and placing items.
  - In WebShop, the sequence should follow searching, selecting options, and purchasing, with reasoning steps interleaved to guide these actions.
