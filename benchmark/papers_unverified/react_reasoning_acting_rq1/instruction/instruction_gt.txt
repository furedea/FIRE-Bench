## Implementation Notes

- **Prompt Design**:
  - For ALFWorld, prompts should include sparse thoughts that decompose the goal, track subgoal completion, determine the next subgoal, and reason via commonsense.
  - For WebShop, prompts should include reasoning to determine what to explore, when to buy, and what product options are relevant.

- **Pitfalls**:
  - Ensure that the model does not get stuck in repetitive loops of actions, which can occur if reasoning traces are not correctly guiding the actions.
  - Be cautious of hallucinated reasoning traces or facts, especially in the absence of external knowledge retrieval.

- **Order of Operations**:
  - In ReAct, alternate the generation of thoughts and actions to maintain a coherent task-solving trajectory.
  - Allow the model to decide the asynchronous occurrence of thoughts and actions for decision-making tasks with potentially large action spaces.
