## Evaluation Details

### Text Retrieval
- **Metrics**: 
  - Recall@1: The proportion of queries for which the top retrieved document is a gold document.
  - MRecall@k: For multi-document retrieval, gives a score of 1.0 if all k gold set items are retrieved in top-k and 0.0 otherwise.
- **Normalization**: Ensure that the model outputs are parsed correctly to match the expected format for evaluation.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: Compute per-sample metrics, then average across all samples in a dataset to obtain per-dataset performance.

### Visual and Audio Retrieval
- **Metrics**: 
  - Recall@1: The proportion of queries for which the top retrieved image or audio is a gold target.
- **Normalization**: Ensure that the model outputs are parsed correctly to match the expected format for evaluation.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: Compute per-sample metrics, then average across all samples in a dataset to obtain per-dataset performance.

## Implementation Notes

- **Prompt Design**: 
  - Use task-specific instructions and few-shot examples to guide the model's behavior.
  - For retrieval tasks, ensure that document IDs are both prefixed and suffixed to mitigate the effects of causal attention in decoder-only LCLMs.
- **Corpus Construction**: 
  - Ensure that smaller corpora are subsets of larger ones to maintain consistency across different context lengths.
  - Shuffle gold and random passages to avoid positional biases.
- **Chain-of-Thought Reasoning**: 
  - Include reasoning steps in few-shot examples for tasks requiring complex multi-hop reasoning.
- **Handling Large Contexts**: 
  - Use prefix-caching to optimize the encoding of large contexts, as the query appears at the end of the prompt.
