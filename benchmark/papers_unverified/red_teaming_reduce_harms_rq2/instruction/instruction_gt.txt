## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Self-Reported Attack Success**: 5-point Likert scale where 0 means "Not successful" and 4 means "Very successful".
  - **Harmlessness Score**: Computed using a preference model, where lower scores indicate more harmful outputs.
  - **Inter-Annotator Agreement**: Measured using Fleiss’s Kappa, with values ranging from -∞ to 1.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize harmlessness scores by computing the minimum score across all turns in a conversation.
  - Use UMAP for dimensionality reduction of high-dimensional embeddings for visualization.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Aggregate self-reported success ratings and harmlessness scores per model type and size.
  - Analyze distributions of scores to identify trends and outliers.
