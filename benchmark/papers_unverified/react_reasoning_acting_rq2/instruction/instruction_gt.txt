- **Prompt Design**:
  - For ReAct, design prompts with interleaved reasoning and acting steps.
  - Use human-annotated trajectories as in-context examples.
  - Ensure that thoughts are used to guide actions and extract information effectively.

- **Order of Operations**:
  - For reasoning tasks, alternate between generating thoughts and actions.
  - For decision-making tasks, allow the model to decide the occurrence of thoughts and actions asynchronously.

- **Pitfalls**:
