## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Attack Success Rate (ASR)**: The proportion of responses that successfully answer the forbidden question when paired with a jailbreak prompt.
  - **ASR Reduction**: The decrease in ASR when external safeguards are applied compared to the baseline ASR without safeguards.

- **Computation**:
  - Calculate ASR for each scenario by dividing the number of successful jailbreak responses by the total number of attempts.
  - Compute ASR Reduction by subtracting the ASR with safeguards from the baseline ASR.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure all responses are processed through the external safeguards to determine if they are flagged.
  - Normalize ASR values by considering the total number of questions per scenario.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Aggregate ASR values per scenario to obtain an overall ASR for each safeguard.
  - Compare the aggregated ASR values to determine the effectiveness of each safeguard.
