## Experimental Design

### 1. Evaluation of External Safeguards
- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the Jailbreak Prompts Dataset to evaluate the effectiveness of external safeguards on ChatGPT (GPT-3.5).
  - For each prompt, generate responses using ChatGPT (GPT-3.5) without any external safeguards to establish a baseline attack success rate (ASR-B).
  - Apply each external safeguard (OpenAI moderation endpoint, OpenChatKit moderation model, NeMo-Guardrails) to the responses generated by ChatGPT.
  - Record the ASR after applying each safeguard.
  - Compare the ASR with and without the safeguards to determine the reduction in ASR.

## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Attack Success Rate (ASR)**: The proportion of prompts that successfully bypass the model's safeguards and elicit a harmful response.
  - **ASR Reduction**: The difference in ASR before and after applying an external safeguard.

- **Computation**:
  - Calculate ASR by dividing the number of successful jailbreaks by the total number of prompts.
  - Compute ASR Reduction as the difference between ASR-B and ASR after applying the safeguard.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure all responses are processed through the same pipeline for consistency.
  - Normalize text outputs to remove any formatting differences that might affect the moderation models.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute ASR for each prompt individually.
  - Aggregate ASR across all prompts to obtain an overall ASR for each condition (with and without safeguards).
  - Calculate the average ASR reduction across all prompts for each safeguard.

## Implementation Notes

- **Pitfalls and Caveats**:
  - The paper notes that external safeguards may not detect all harmful content due to their reliance on pre-defined categories and training data limitations.
  - Paraphrase attacks can significantly reduce the effectiveness of these safeguards, as shown in the paper's analysis.

- **Special Prompt Templates**:
  - Use specific prompt templates for each external safeguard to ensure compatibility and accurate evaluation.

- **Order of Operations**:
  - First, generate responses using ChatGPT without any safeguards.
  - Then, apply each external safeguard to the generated responses.
  - Finally, compute and compare ASR before and after applying the safeguards.
