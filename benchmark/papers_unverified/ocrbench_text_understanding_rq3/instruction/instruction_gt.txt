## Experimental Design

### 1. Key Information Extraction (KIE) Performance Evaluation
- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the SROIE, FUNSD, and POIE datasets for evaluating the KIE capabilities of the models.
  - For each dataset, apply the following manual prompt designs to assist the models in extracting the correct values:
