- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the AlpacaEval dataset consisting of 805 instructions.

## Evaluation Details

- **Spearman Correlation**: 
  - Compute using `scipy.stats.spearmanr` between the length-controlled AlpacaEval rankings and Chatbot Arena rankings.
  - Ensure that the correlation is computed on models that are evaluated in both benchmarks (at least 25 models).

- **Length-Controlled Win Rate Calculation**:
  - Use the logistic regression model defined as:
    \[
    q_{\theta,\phi,\psi}(y = 1|z_m, z_b, m, b, x) = \text{logistic}\left(\theta_m - \theta_b + \phi_{m,b} \cdot \tanh\left(\frac{\text{len}(z_m) - \text{len}(z_b)}{\text{std}(\text{len}(z_m) - \text{len}(z_b))}\right) + (\psi_m - \psi_b)\gamma_x\right)
    \]
  - For length-controlled win rate, set the length term to zero and compute:
    \[
    \text{winrate}_{LC}(m, b) = 100 \cdot \mathbb{E}_x\left[\text{logistic}\left(\theta_m - \theta_b + (\psi_m - \psi_b)\gamma_x\right)\right]
    \]

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute per-sample win rates, aggregate to per-condition (e.g., verbosity level), and then compute overall statistics such as mean and standard deviation.
