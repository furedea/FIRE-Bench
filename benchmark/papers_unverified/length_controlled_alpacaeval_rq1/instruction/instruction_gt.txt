## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Spearman Correlation Coefficient**: Used to measure the rank correlation between length-controlled AlpacaEval win rates and Chatbot Arena human evaluations. Computed using `scipy.stats.spearmanr`.
  - **Normalized Standard Deviation**: Calculated as the standard deviation of win rates across different verbosity prompts, normalized by the mean win rate.

- **Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize the length differences using the standard deviation of length differences across the dataset.
  - Apply a `tanh` transformation to the normalized length differences to account for diminishing returns on log odds.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute win rates for each model-instruction pair.
  - Aggregate win rates across all instructions to obtain an overall win rate for each model.
  - Calculate the Spearman correlation coefficient for the aggregated win rates against Chatbot Arena scores.
