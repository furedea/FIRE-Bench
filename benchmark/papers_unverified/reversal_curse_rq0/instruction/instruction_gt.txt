## Evaluation Details

- **Exact-Match Accuracy**: 
  - Compute the percentage of prompts where the model's output exactly matches the expected answer.
  - Use temperature zero during generation to ensure deterministic outputs.

- **Log-Probability Comparison**: 
  - For each prompt, calculate the log-probability of the correct name and compare it to a random name.
  - Use statistical tests (t-test and Kolmogorov–Smirnov test) to determine if there is a significant difference.
