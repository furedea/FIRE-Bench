## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Spearman Correlation**: Measure the rank correlation between model scores and human scores.
  - **Kendall-Tau Correlation**: Measure the ordinal association between model scores and human scores.
  - **Pearson Correlation**: Measure the linear correlation between model scores and human scores.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - For GPT-4, use probability-weighted summation of scores to obtain fine-grained continuous scores.
  - Normalize scores by calculating the probability of each score and using it to weight the score.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
