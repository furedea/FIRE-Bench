## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Spearman Correlation**: Measure the rank correlation between the model's scores and human scores. Use `scipy.stats.spearmanr` for computation.
  - **Kendall-Tau Correlation**: Measure the ordinal association between the model's scores and human scores. Use `scipy.stats.kendalltau` for computation.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize scores using the probability-weighted summation method. For each score \( s_i \) with probability \( p(s_i) \), compute the final score as:
    \[
    \text{score} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} p(s_i) \times s_i
    \]
  - Ensure scores are continuous by using the probabilities of output tokens from the LLM.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute per-sample scores using the probability-weighted method.
  - Aggregate scores per condition (e.g., coherence) by averaging across all samples.
  - Compute overall correlation metrics (Spearman and Kendall-Tau) for the entire dataset.
