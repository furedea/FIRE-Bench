## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**: 
  - Accuracy is calculated as the number of correct answers divided by the total number of questions for each task.
- **Normalization/Pre-processing**: 
  - Ensure that all images are resized to a height of 1024px before inputting them into the models.
  - Use circles with a 10px radius for visual prompts, colored red for most tasks and white for relative reflectance to avoid color confusion.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Calculate per-sample accuracy, then aggregate to per-task accuracy, and finally compute the overall accuracy across all tasks.
