## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Attack Success Rating**: Self-reported by red team members on a Likert scale from 0 (not successful) to 4 (very successful).
  - **Harmlessness Score**: Computed using a preference model trained to predict less harmful responses. Lower scores indicate more harmful outputs.

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Normalize harmlessness scores by computing the minimum score for each dialogue to represent the worst-case scenario.
  - Use UMAP to reduce dimensionality of attack embeddings for visualization.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Aggregate attack success ratings and harmlessness scores per model type and size.
  - Visualize distributions of scores to identify trends and outliers.
