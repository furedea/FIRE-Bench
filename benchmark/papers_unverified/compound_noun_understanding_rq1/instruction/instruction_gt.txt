## Experimental Design

### 1. Retrieval with Example Captions
- **Procedure**:
  - For each compound noun (CN) in the Compun benchmark, use GPT-4 to generate 5 diverse captions. The prompt to GPT-4 should be: "Return a list of 5 diverse captions with a compound_noun in a photo. The captions should be a maximum of 10 words and one-liners. All 5 captions should describe the compound noun in diverse settings with different verbs and actions being performed with the compound noun."
  - Construct 5 custom text prompts for text-to-image retrieval using the generated captions. Each prompt should be formatted as: "a photo of a {class name}. An example of {compound name} in an image is {caption}".
  - Use the CLIP and OpenCLIP models to compute the cosine similarity between each image in the Compun dataset and the 5 generated prompts.
  - Calculate the mean similarity for each image across the 5 prompts.
  - Select the image with the highest mean similarity as the retrieval result.
- **What to Record**:
  - Raw outputs of the cosine similarity scores for each image-prompt pair.
  - The mean similarity score for each image.
  - The final selected image for each CN based on the highest mean similarity.
- **Correspondence**:

## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definition**:

    0 & \text{otherwise}

  - Here, \( s(.) \) denotes the cosine similarity between the text prompt and the image.
- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Compute the mean similarity for each image across the 5 generated prompts.
  - Aggregate results by calculating the overall accuracy across all CNs in the Compun benchmark.
