### Metric Definitions and Computation
- **Accuracy**: 
  - Compute the accuracy for each task as the percentage of correctly answered questions out of the total questions for that task.
  - Use the formula: \( \text{Accuracy} = \frac{\text{Number of Correct Answers}}{\text{Total Number of Questions}} \times 100 \).

- **Model Outputs**:

## Implementation Notes

### Key Pitfalls and Caveats
- **Visual Prompting**: 
  - The size and color of visual prompts (e.g., circles) can significantly affect model performance. Use 10px red circles for consistency, as this was found optimal in the paper.
- **Multiple-Image Inputs**: 
  - For models that do not support multiple-image inputs, concatenate images horizontally with a black margin in between.

### Special Prompt Templates
- **Prompt Templates**: 
  - Use specific prompt templates for each task as described in the paper, ensuring that both visual and textual components are included.

### Order of Operations

- **Output Parsing**:
