## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definition**: 
  - Tokenization Premium: \( \text{Premium} = \frac{|t(s_A)|}{|t(s_B)|} \), where \( |t(s_A)| \) is the tokenization length of sentence \( s_A \) in language A, and \( |t(s_B)| \) is the tokenization length of the same sentence in language B.
- **Normalization**: 
  - Ensure that all tokenization lengths are computed consistently across models and languages.
- **Aggregation Strategy**: 
  - Compute tokenization premiums per sentence, then average across all sentences to obtain a per-language premium.
  - Compare premiums across models to assess overall tokenization parity.
