## Experimental Design

### 1. LLM-based Choice Extraction Analysis
- **Procedure**: 
  - Use the MMBench dataset to evaluate the effectiveness of LLM-based choice extraction.
  - For each multiple-choice question, first attempt to extract the choice label from VLM predictions using heuristic matching.
  - If heuristic matching fails, employ GPT-4 to extract the choice label by providing the question, choices, and model prediction to the LLM, and request it to align the prediction with one of the given choices.
  - Conduct a comparison between the heuristic matching and LLM-based extraction.
  - Use a subset of inference records where heuristic matching fails to validate LLM extraction against human annotations.
- **What to Record**:

### 2. CircularEval Strategy Evaluation
- **Procedure**: 
  - Implement the CircularEval strategy on the MMBench dataset.
  - For each question, perform multiple inference passes with circularly shifted choices.

## Evaluation Details

### LLM-based Choice Extraction
- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Alignment Rate**: The percentage of cases where the LLM-extracted choice matches human annotations.
  - **Accuracy Improvement**: The increase in accuracy when using LLM-based extraction compared to heuristic matching.
- **Normalization/Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure all model outputs are formatted consistently before passing to the LLM for extraction.
- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Calculate per-sample alignment and accuracy, then aggregate to determine overall improvement.

### CircularEval Strategy
- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Top-1 Accuracy**: The percentage of questions correctly answered in all circular passes.
  - **Accuracy Drop**: The difference in accuracy between CircularEval and VanillaEval.
- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Aggregate accuracy across all questions to determine overall performance under each evaluation strategy.

## Implementation Notes

- **Pitfalls/Caveats**:
  - Ensure that the LLM used for choice extraction is properly instructed to avoid external inference beyond the provided options.
  - Be aware of potential content moderation in proprietary VLMs that may affect evaluation results.
- **Prompt Templates**:
  - Use specific prompt templates for LLM-based choice extraction and MMBench-CN translation to ensure consistency.
- **Order of Operations**:
  - Perform heuristic matching before LLM-based extraction to minimize unnecessary LLM calls.
  - Conduct CircularEval only after confirming the model's ability to handle multiple-choice formats.
