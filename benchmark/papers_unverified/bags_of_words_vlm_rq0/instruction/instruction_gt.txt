## Evaluation Details

### Metric Definitions
- **Accuracy**: The proportion of correctly identified captions (original vs. permuted or correct vs. incorrect) out of the total number of test cases.
- **Macro Accuracy**: Average accuracy across different types of relations or attributes, treating each type equally regardless of the number of samples.

### Pre-processing and Normalization
- Ensure that all captions are grammatically correct and semantically plausible to avoid biasing the model towards one choice based on language quality.

### Aggregation Strategy
- **Per-sample**: Record the model's choice for each test case.
- **Per-condition**: Calculate accuracy for each type of relation or attribute separately.
- **Overall**: Compute the overall accuracy and macro accuracy across all test cases.

## Implementation Notes

- **Prompt Templates**: Use specific templates for generating correct and incorrect captions, ensuring consistency across all test cases.
- **Output Parsing**: Implement logic to parse the model's output and determine which caption was selected.
- **Order of Operations**: Ensure that the model is presented with the image and all caption options simultaneously to avoid any order effects in decision-making.
