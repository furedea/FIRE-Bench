## Experimental Design

### 1. Visual Genome Attribution and Relation Tasks
- **Procedure**: 
  - For the Visual Genome Attribution task, present the model with images and two captions: one correct and one with swapped attributes (e.g., "the crouched cat and the open door" vs. "the open cat and the crouched door").
  - For the Visual Genome Relation task, present the model with images and two captions: one correct and one with swapped relations (e.g., "the dog is behind the tree" vs. "the tree is behind the dog").
  - Use the Visual Genome dataset to extract images with 48 relations and 117 unique attribute pairs, resulting in 23,937 relation test cases and 28,748 attribution test cases.
  - Ensure that the bounding box containing both objects is extracted to minimize distractions.
- **What to Record**: 
  - Record the model's choice between the correct and swapped captions.
  - Calculate accuracy as the percentage of correct choices.
- **Correspondence**: 
  - Figure 1 and Table 2 in the paper.

### 2. COCO Order and Flickr30k Order Tasks
- **Procedure**: 
  - For each image, present the model with the original caption and four systematically permuted captions (e.g., shuffling nouns and adjectives, shuffling all words, etc.).
  - Use the COCO and Flickr30k datasets to generate these permutations.
  - Ensure that part-of-speech tagging is used to perform the perturbations.
- **What to Record**: 
  - Record the model's choice among the five captions.
  - Calculate accuracy as the percentage of times the model selects the original caption.
- **Correspondence**: 
  - Figure 1 and Table 5 in the paper.

## Evaluation Details

- **Metric Definitions**:
  - **Accuracy**: Defined as the number of correct selections (original or correct caption) divided by the total number of test cases.
  - **Chance Level**: For the Visual Genome tasks, chance level is 50% (two options). For the COCO and Flickr30k Order tasks, chance level is 20% (five options).

- **Normalization and Pre-processing**:
  - Ensure that all captions are grammatically correct and semantically plausible, even when swapped or permuted.
  - Use Spacy for part-of-speech tagging to ensure accurate perturbations.

- **Aggregation Strategy**:
  - Calculate per-sample accuracy, then aggregate to obtain per-condition accuracy (e.g., spatial relations, verbs).
  - Report overall accuracy for each task.

## Implementation Notes

- **Pitfalls and Caveats**:
  - Ensure that the bounding boxes in the Visual Genome tasks are correctly extracted to focus on the relevant objects.
  - Be cautious of symmetric relations (e.g., "near", "next to") which should be removed from the dataset.

- **Prompt Templates**:
  - Use specific templates for generating captions in the Visual Genome tasks: "the [attribute 1] [object 1] and the [attribute 2] [object 2]" for correct captions and "the [attribute 2] [object 1] and the [attribute 1] [object 2]" for swapped captions.

- **Order of Operations**:
